Store Policies

Simple policies for shipping, payments, and support.

These pages help customers know what to expect before placing an order.

Shipping

Orders are usually dispatched within 24 to 48 hours. Delivery windows shown on the shop page are estimates and can vary by location.

Shipping is free for orders above Rs 3,000. Smaller orders use a flat shipping fee calculated at checkout.

Payments

Customers can choose cash on delivery or online payment through Razorpay once keys are configured on the backend.

Online orders are verified on the server before being marked paid.

Cancellations And Returns

If an order has not shipped yet, contact us as soon as possible for cancellation help. For product issues after delivery, use the contact page and include your order number.

Support

For gifting questions, bulk requests, or order support, send a message from the contact page and we will save it through the website backend.
